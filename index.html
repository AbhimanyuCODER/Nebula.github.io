About Us

Description Of Club

NEBULA is established by the G.L. Bajaj Institute of Technology and Management under the Innovation cell. It fundamentally embraces on three columns that are Enterpreneurship , Innovation and Incubation. The principle moto of the club is to advance development among the Students regarding startup and business and so forth by giving guidance, counselling and appropriate practical sessions to embellish their ideas.

About Us
Our Pedestal

INNOVATION

Well with commonly having an image of risk takers and a person of uncertain decisions, an Entrepreneur consumes a vital part in any developing or developed economy. They are the people who structure a business, bears a large portion of the danger and appreciates the greater part of the prize. Our club guides & encourages those students who are looking to form such a start-up and want to make their way in life. We knock behind every door of possibility that can shape the future of several quite diverse minds.

INCUBATION

Incubation consists of supporting or fostering a beginning phase company/organization through various development stages. This process permits an entrepreneur to safeguard his funds and gain outer counseling to advance his growth. They financially manage the beginner, offer them guidance up to a greater extent and help them create an alliances beftween startups. Our club precisely keeps these young minds updated about any opportunity that they can grab to make their idea manufacturable and marketable in a true sense.

ENTREPRENEURSHIP

Now when we talk about Entrepreneurship, Innovation can be said to be its soul. In today's greatly competitive world, innovative ideas are what will separate you from a mob mentality. Innovation is the process that turns an idea from arguable into marketable form. It takes continuous attempts and endless rehearsals before getting accepted by an organization. We,in NEBULA Club, try every way possible to make that happen and also provide suitable resources to an Entrepreneur so that he/she doesn't feel lacking behind from an individual since they all have entirely different ideas.
